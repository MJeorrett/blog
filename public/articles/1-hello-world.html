Welcome to 'Nine Bits in a Byte'

02/02/2018

Hi, I'm Matthew and this is my blog called Nine Bits in a Byte (I'll explain why in a future article).

I am Developer Evangelist at Wallet.Services where I mostly write C# and typescript. We are developing a blockchain-as-a-service platform so I get to play with blockchains sometimes which is awesome!

I have started this blog as a channel for exploring different things from my day to day work. Most of what I am paid for is coding in high level languages and so here I want to explore lower level stuff; I want to get close to the metal!

This page is quite basic (as you may have noticed!). I have started with a really simple a setup: a node back end running an express server and no framework or templating. My plan is to build up this site and get back to the basics of front end development with the aim of getting a deeper understanding of what is going on.

I'm going to find a way to directly link the source code of this site with the content so that this blog is about the development of the blog: inception!. While I head off to see if I can get this inception idea rolling I'll leave you with a picture; my marketing colleague Blockchain Beyonce told me that blogs with pictures get more readers...
